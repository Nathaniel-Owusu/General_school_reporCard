Admissions Process

Start your journey at General School. We make the enrollment process straightforward and welcoming.

Welcome Prospective Students

General School is currently accepting applications for the upcoming academic year across all levels. Below, you will find all the required steps and information to complete your child's admission seamlessly.

1

Obtain Application

Purchase an application form directly from the school administration office located in Techiman-Takofiano.

2

Submit Documents

Fill out the physical application form completely and submit it along with all the required supporting documents.

3

Enrollment & Portal

Upon acceptance, pay the admission fees and receive your secure login credentials to the Student Portal.

Required Documents

Ensure you have copies of the following documents ready when submitting your physical application:

  • Completed Admission Form
  • Copy of Birth Certificate or Passport
  • Four (4) recent passport-sized photographs
  • Previous School Reports/Transcripts (if applicable)
  • Immunization Record / Medical Report

Admissions Support

If you have any questions about the admission process, tuition fees, or would like to schedule a campus tour before enrolling, our dedicated admissions team is ready to assist you.
