Academic Programs

Fostering intellectual growth, critical thinking, and a lifelong love for learning through a comprehensive and dynamic curriculum.

Our Curriculum

Structured for Success

Kindergarten (KG)

Early childhood education focused on foundational literacy, numeracy, and creative arts through play-based learning.

  • Language & Literacy
  • Numeracy
  • Environmental Studies
  • Creative Arts

Primary School

A robust six-year program building strong academic pillars in core subjects while encouraging critical thinking.

  • English Language
  • Mathematics
  • Science & Tech
  • Ghanaian Language

Junior High (JHS)

Three years of intense preparation for secondary education, focusing on advanced concepts and career orientation.

  • Core Mathematics
  • Integrated Science
  • Computing
  • Career Technology

Fair & Transparent Grading

Our student evaluation is based on a modern, standard grading scale to ensure students clearly understand their academic standing.

The End of Term Terminal Report Sheets are calculated automatically based on 30% Class Scores (Continuous Assessment) and 70% Exam Scores.
